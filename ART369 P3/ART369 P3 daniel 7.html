Daniel Macri
Director of Operations

Danny is an Environmental Management candidate at the Yale School of Forestry and Environmental Studies. Prior to FES Danny supported environmental and agriculture USAID programs in Asia where he lived in Tajikistan for a year. He also studied abroad in Italy and served as a Jesuit Volunteer in Belize. At FES, Danny is interested in studying how industrial by-products and other types of waste can be transformed into assets. Danny enjoys video editing, botany, airports, brewing, travel, and eating. He also holds the 2014 title of Best Chili among the Yale Professional and Graduate schools.
